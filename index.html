02 · About

Behind the lens: how she directs, shoots and edits projects from first message to final export.

Artist statement

Every image is a tiny universe: light, colour and expression meeting in a fraction of a second. My work lives in that tension between cinematic and familiar – stylised enough to feel intentional, honest enough to feel like you.

From first moodboard to final export, I build spaces where people forget the camera. That’s when the good stuff happens: the almost-laugh, the unposed glance, the movement in-between beats.

Whether it’s an intimate portrait session, a chaotic live show, or a brand campaign, my priority is always the same – tell the most truthful version of your story, with visuals bold enough to cut through the noise.

Tools & workflow

Shooting on digital mirrorless systems, grading with a film-inspired palette, and cutting in modern editing suites – built for social, streaming and large-format prints.
